Why does this website exist?

For a long time Gus was just like any other large ginger cat from a good family; happy, but for whatever reason self-doubt set in. His self-doubt manifests as him questioning the nature of his existence and these questions center around the premise of this website; is he a cat?

My efforts to reassure him that he is in fact a cat have been fruitless. He's fully aware that I'm not an impartial judge so this poll of internet strangers is intended to prove to him that people other than me believe that he is a cat.
